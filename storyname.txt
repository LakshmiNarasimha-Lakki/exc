Coming to the setting of the story.It is a fantasy story happened a long time ago,In which there are seven kingdoms under 1 king Robert Baratheon.
The Northern Border has a wall, and there is a castle called Castle black. The people there are sworn to protect the wall,if any ones disobeys their promise then they will be executed.
The execution is done by Lord Eddard Stark , Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North.
